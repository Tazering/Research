# ***SDSS DR12***

## **Features**

- `ra`: right ascension, celestial equivalent of longitude (degrees)
- `dec`: declination, celestial equivalent of latitude (degrees)
- `u`: ultraviolet, $\lambda = 3543$ 
- `g`: green, $\lambda = 4770$
- `r`: red, $\lambda = 6231$
- `i`: near infrared, $\lambda = 7625$
- `z`: infrared, $\lambda 9134$
